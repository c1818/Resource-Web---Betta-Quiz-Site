Why this website exists

This website was created as a beginner-friendly resource to help people think more carefully before buying a betta fish. It is built around a quiz and supporting information pages that focus on important care topics such as tank size, setup, water conditions, maintenance, feeding, emergency planning, and general readiness.

Instead of encouraging impulse buying, the goal of the project is to slow that process down and help users understand whether they are actually prepared. Betta fish are often treated as easy “starter” pets, even though proper care still requires planning, equipment, time, and basic knowledge.

The quiz is meant to act as a starting point. It helps users reflect on whether they have the right setup and whether there are things they still need to learn before buying a betta fish. The Learn / Support page gives more detailed information connected to those topics, while the References section shows the sources behind the information included on the site.

Rather than framing betta fish as low-maintenance purchases, this website encourages users to see them as living animals that deserve proper care and preparation.
